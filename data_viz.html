Data Visualizations and Insights



Price Average per Neighborhood Groups


   This map chart of New York City is grouped by different neighborhoods: Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, and Staten Island. From the map we can clearly see that Manhattan has the highest prices for Airbnbs.

Insight: Clear price difference between each neighborhood group.


Price Average per Neighborhood


   This map chart of New York City is seperated by the different neighborhoods. We see interesting results from this chart that we didn't expect. Some neighborhoods have missing data so they are grayed out.

Insight: We see that Brooklyn Heights and Cobble Hill have a high average price comapred to all other neighborhoods.



Price Per Night Distribution in Manhattan


   Looking at the Airbnbs in Manhattan, it seems that although there are some very expensive rooms, there are enough affordable rooms priced between $0~100 US Dollars.

Insight: Manhattan has a wide range of price but a decent amount are in our friend's price range.



Top 5 Most Reviewed Under $80


   This map chart of New York City is dotted based on the top 5 most reviewed Airbnbs under $80 a night. We see that 4 of the 5 are in the Central Harlem North-Polo Grounds neighborhood, but 1 of the 5 is in East Village.

Insight: We recommend the East Village Airbnb based on the reviews, location, and price.



Distribution of Airbnbs in Each Neighborhood


   Manhattan and Brooklyn have the most number of Airbnb units available, with Manhattan having about 200 more. There are not a lot of housing options in the other neighborhoods, so it would be recommended that our friend finds housing in either Manhattan or Brooklyn.

Insight: There are more options available in Manhattan and Brooklyn.





Dataset used: Dgomonov, New York City Airbnb Open Data - Airbnb listings and metrics in NYC, NY, USA (2019), 2019, Kaggle, Version 3, https://www.kaggle.com/dgomonov/new-york-city-airbnb-open-data
